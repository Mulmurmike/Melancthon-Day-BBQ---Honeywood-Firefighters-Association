Melancthon Day Community BBQ

4th Annual Melancthon Day  ·  Dufferin County  ·  Honeywood Firefighters Association

🍔🌭 About This Event

Join us at the 5th Annual Melancthon Day in Dufferin County! The BBQ is hosted by the Honeywood Firefighters Association, part of the Mulmur–Melancthon Fire Department. Come out, enjoy great food, and support the volunteers who protect our rural community.

Burgers and hot dogs are served — no set price. All we ask is a donation from the heart. Every dollar goes directly back into keeping our community safe. Every neighbour is welcome — please enjoy yourselves!
